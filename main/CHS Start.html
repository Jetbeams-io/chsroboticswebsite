    CHS Robotics


    Who are we?

    CHS stands for Carroll High School, which is part of the NACS district. Our team is composed of many students from all grades, 9th through 12th. We elect a different student to be the leader every year, but everything is coordinated by Gary Platt, a PLTW teacher here at Carroll.

    What do we do?

    Our team builds and codes robots to compete in the YBOT Challenge every year. Each challenge requires a different type of robot to complete the task successfully. We design and build our robot using VEX Robotics parts, and we program it using ROBOTC.


    You can learn more about YBOT at www.youthbot.org or you can click that beautiful, glowing, rainbow "About" button for more information about us!
